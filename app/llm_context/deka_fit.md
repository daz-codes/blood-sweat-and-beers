# Deka Fit

## Format
Deka Fit is a 10-zone functional fitness competition. Athletes complete each zone in order, with a **500m run before every zone** (10 runs total = 5km of running spread throughout the event).

**Sequence:** Run 500m → Zone 1 → Run 500m → Zone 2 → … → Run 500m → Zone 10

## The 10 Zones

| Zone | Exercise | Reps / Distance | Men | Women |
|------|----------|-----------------|-----|-------|
| 1 | RAM Reverse Lunges (alternating) | 30 reps (15/leg) | 25kg | 15kg |
| 2 | Row | 500m | — | — |
| 3 | Box Jump / Step Over | 20 reps | 24" box | 24" box |
| 4 | Med Ball Sit-up Throw | 25 reps | 9kg | 6.5kg |
| 5 | SkiErg | 500m | — | — |
| 6 | Farmer's Carry | 100m | 27kg each hand | 18kg each hand |
| 7 | Air Bike | 25 calories | — | — |
| 8 | Dead Ball Yoke Over | 20 reps (10/side) | 27kg | 18kg |
| 9 | Sled Push / Pull | 100m (push 10m + pull 10m × 5) | resistance varies | resistance varies |
| 10 | RAM Weighted Burpees | 20 reps | 20kg | 10kg |

## Training Philosophy
Training sessions prepare athletes for Deka Fit — they are **not** mini copies of the event. Build aerobic capacity, running economy under fatigue, grip endurance, and leg power.

Sessions do **not** need all 10 zones — focused blocks of 2–5 zones are more effective. Complementary exercises are welcome: e.g. goblet squats for lunge endurance, DB rows for rowing muscles, KB swings for posterior chain. Avoid exercises unrelated to the event's demands.
