# Dirty Dozen

## Format
The Dirty Dozen is a 12-movement/event functional fitness competition. Competitors complete 12 different challenges that collectively test broad physical fitness across multiple domains.

*Specific movements and format may vary by event organiser — verify with the specific Dirty Dozen event being trained for.*

## Movement Domains
A Dirty Dozen competition typically covers:
- **Monostructural cardio:** Running, rowing, SkiErg, Bike Erg, assault bike
- **Olympic/barbell:** Clean, snatch, deadlift, thruster, push press
- **Gymnastics/bodyweight:** Burpees, pull-ups, muscle-ups, handstand push-ups, box jumps, toes-to-bar
- **Loaded carries:** Farmers carry, sandbag, sled push/pull
- **Kettlebell:** Swings, goblet squats, single-arm press
- **Odd object:** Sandbag, atlas stones, D-ball

## Training Principles
- **Broad fitness is the priority** — no single weakness can be hidden across 12 events
- **12 events = 12 different energy demands** — some are sprints, others are sustained efforts
- **Pacing variety** — learn to go maximum for 30 seconds and also pace for 5 minutes
- **Skill development** — gymnastic and technical movements (muscle-ups, snatches) reward efficiency
- **Recovery between events** — if there is rest between events, use it fully

## Common Training Session Types
1. **Mixed modal WODs** — varied movements in AMRAP, EMOM, or chipper formats
2. **Gymnastics skill work** — pull-ups, ring muscle-ups, handstand push-ups, toes-to-bar
3. **Barbell cycling** — high-rep clean, snatch, deadlift under fatigue
4. **Sprint conditioning** — short intense intervals across multiple modalities (row, bike, ski, run)
5. **Aerobic base** — longer moderate-effort sessions across rowing, running, skiing
6. **Loaded carry circuits** — farmers, sandbag, sled push/pull in rotation
7. **Benchmark WODs** — known workouts (Fran, Helen, Cindy) as fitness tests

## Example Sessions

**Session: Mixed Modal AMRAP**
- 20 min AMRAP:
  - 10 Pull-ups
  - 15 KB Swings (24kg M / 16kg W)
  - 20 Box Jumps
  - 200m Run

**Session: Sprint Medley**
- 4 rounds, 1 min rest between each:
  - 250m Row (max effort)
  - 250m SkiErg (max effort)
  - 250m Bike Erg (max effort)

**Session: Barbell + Carry**
- 5 rounds:
  - 7 Power Cleans (moderate weight)
  - 50m Farmers Carry
  - 10 Burpee Box Jump Overs
  / 90s rest
